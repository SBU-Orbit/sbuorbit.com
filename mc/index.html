Commands

  • /back - Teleports you to your locaiton prior to tp/warp/death. Costs $25 / use.
  • /sethome [name] - Sets your home to your current location. Costs $25 / use
  • /ranks - Lists ranks.
  • /rankup - Rankup to next rank.
  • /kit [kit] - Obtains the specified kit or views all available kits.
  • /showkit <kit> - Preview kit.
  • /rankup - Rankup to next rank.
  • /nickname <nickname|off> - Change your nickname
  • /feed - SB+ Refill your hunger bar. Costs $40 / use
  • /workbench - SB+ Opens a crafting table. Costs $0.10 / use
  • /enderchest - SB+ Opens an enderchest.
  • /condense - VET Condenses items into more compact blocks.
